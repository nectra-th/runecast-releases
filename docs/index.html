Features

Everything You Need. Nothing You Don't.

From simple clicks to complex conditional logic — RuneCast covers every automation scenario.

Smart Click

Image-based clicking that adapts to UI changes. RuneCast finds the image wherever it moves — and can wait for it to appear.

Drag & Drop

Match source image, drag to destination. Automate inventory management, card games, and more.

Conditional Logic

If image A is found, click image B. Build smart branching workflows that adapt to screen state.

HotKey Sequences

When an image is detected, fire complex key combinations. Automate skill rotations and inputs.

Multi-Template

Match multiple images simultaneously. Handle dynamic UIs where elements change appearance.

Background Mode

Run automation silently without moving your real mouse. Browse and work while RuneCast handles everything.

LINE Notifications

Get push alerts on your phone when automation goes idle or repeats. Control play/stop remotely from LINE chat.

Priority Engine

Mark critical templates as priority for synchronous execution. Adaptive thread-pool scales automatically with load.

Live Dashboard

Monitor all profiles from the launcher — see CPU, memory, uptime, and automation state in real time.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is RuneCast safe to use? ▶

Yes. RuneCast uses pixel-level image recognition only — it never reads or injects into game memory. It's a single .exe with no installer, no registry modifications, and no background services. Clean and portable — delete it and it's gone.

Is RuneCast really free? ▶

Yes. RuneCast is currently in active development and every feature is 100% free during this period. Users who download and use it now receive a Pioneer License — meaning all features remain free for you forever, even after official launch and future pricing changes.

What is the Pioneer License? ▶

The Pioneer License is our way of rewarding early adopters. If you download and use RuneCast during the development phase, you'll keep full access to all features — permanently, at no cost. When RuneCast officially launches with pricing, Pioneer users are grandfathered in with lifetime access. No strings attached.

Will I get banned for using this in games? ▶

RuneCast operates at the pixel level and does not modify game files or memory. However, automating games may violate their Terms of Service. Use responsibly and check each game's policy.

What does "lifetime updates" mean? ▶

Every future update is included at no additional cost. The app automatically checks for updates on startup. Pioneer License holders always get the latest features and improvements — forever, even after launch.

What apps and games does it work with? ▶

RuneCast works with any application that renders to a window on Windows 10/11 — games, browsers, desktop apps, and more.

Can I use my PC while it runs? ▶

Absolutely. Background mode sends clicks and keystrokes directly to the target window without moving your real mouse. You can browse, chat, or work on other tasks simultaneously.

Can I share templates with friends? ▶

Yes! Use the Export/Import feature in the gear menu, or grab community-made templates directly from our Discord's template sharing channel. Templates are bundled into a .runecast file with all images included.

What's on the Discord server? ▶

Our Discord has real-time support from the developer, gameplay automation demo videos, a template sharing channel where you can download setups for popular games, roadmap voting, and early access to beta features including the upcoming Android version.

Can I contribute ideas or features? ▶

Absolutely. Every major feature in RuneCast was suggested by a community member. Join the Discord to vote on the roadmap and suggest new ideas directly to the dev.
